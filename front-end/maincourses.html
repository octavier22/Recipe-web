Chicken and Fish

Ingredients:
fish
• 1 pound cod, haddock, or other firm-fleshed white fish, cut into 1-inch pieces
• Salt and pepper to taste
• 1/4 cup all-purpose flour

• 1 tablespoon baking powder
• 1/2 teaspoon salt
• 1/4 cup milk
Chips
• 1/2 cup vegetable oil for frying
• 2 pounds russet potatoes, peeled and cut into 1/2-inch thick fries
• 1/2 cup vegetable oil for frying
• Salt to taste
2 cups egg noodles

Instructions:
1. Preheat the oven to 200 degrees F (93 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with paper towels.
2. In a shallow bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt. In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk and egg.
3. Dredge the fish in the flour mixture, then dip in the milk mixture.
4. Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Fry the fish in batches until golden brown and cooked through, about 3 to 4 minutes per batch.
5. Transfer the fish to the prepared baking sheet and keep warm in the oven.
6. To make the chips, heat the oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. Fry the chips in batches until golden brown and crispy, about 5 to 7 minutes per batch.
7. Drain the chips on paper towels and sprinkle with salt.
8. To make the tartar sauce, whisk together all of the ingredients in a small bowl.
9. Serve the fish and chips with tartar sauce and malt vinegar.

Lasagna bolognese

Ingredients:
For the Bolognese Sauce:
• 2 tablespoons olive oil
• 1 onion, finely chopped
• 2 carrots, finely chopped
• 2 celery stalks, finely chopped
• 2 cloves garlic, minced
• 1 pound ground beef
• 1/2 pound ground pork
• 1/2 cup dry red wine
• 1 (28-ounce) can crushed tomatoes
• 1 cup beef broth
• 1 teaspoon dried oregano
• 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
• Salt and pepper to taste
For the Béchamel Sauce:

• 1/2 cup unsalted butter
• 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
• 4 cups milk
• 1/2 teaspoon salt
• 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
• 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

For the Lasagna:

• 1 pound lasagna noodles
• 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
• 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Instructions

1. Make the Bolognese Sauce: Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the onion, carrots, celery, and garlic and cook until softened, about 5 minutes. Add the ground beef and pork and cook until browned. Drain off any excess grease.
2. Deglaze the pot with the red wine, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom of the pot. Add the crushed tomatoes, beef broth, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Bring to a simmer and cook, stirring occasionally, for at least 1 hour, or up to 4 hours.
3. Make the Béchamel Sauce: Melt the butter in a medium saucepan over medium heat. Whisk in the flour and cook for 1 minute, whisking constantly. Gradually whisk in the milk, salt, and pepper. Bring to a simmer and cook, stirring occasionally, until thickened, about 5 minutes. Remove from heat and stir in the Parmesan cheese.
4. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
5. Assemble the lasagna: Spread a thin layer of Bolognese sauce on the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Top with a layer of lasagna noodles. Spread a thin layer of Béchamel sauce over the noodles, then sprinkle with Parmesan cheese and mozzarella cheese. Repeat the layers, ending with a layer of sauce and cheese.
6. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for 30 minutes. Uncover and bake for 30 minutes more, or until the lasagna is bubbly and the cheese is melted and golden brown.
7. Let stand for 10 minutes before serving.

CHICKEN SCHNITZEL

INGRIDIENTS:
• 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
• 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
• 2 large eggs, beaten
• 1 1/2 cups panko breadcrumbs
• 1 tablespoon garlic powder
• 1 teaspoon salt

• 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
• Vegetable oil for frying
• Lemon wedges, for serving

STEPS
1. Place the chicken breasts between two pieces of plastic wrap and pound them thin with a meat mallet or the bottom of a heavy pan.
2. In three separate shallow bowls, place the flour, beaten eggs, and panko breadcrumbs. Season the flour with garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
3. Dredge each chicken breast in the flour, then the eggs, and finally the breadcrumbs.
4. Heat about 1/2 inch of vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Fry the chicken breasts in batches until golden brown and crispy on both sides, about 3-4 minutes per batch.
5. Drain the chicken breasts on paper towels and serve immediately with lemon wedges.

Falafel

Ingredients:
• 1 cup dried chickpeas, soaked overnight
• 1/4 cup chopped onion
• 1/4 cup chopped parsley
• 1/4 cup chopped cilantro

• 2 cloves garlic, minced
• 1 teaspoon cumin
• 1 teaspoon coriander
• 1/2 teaspoon salt
• 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
• 1/4 cup flour
• 1/4 cup water
• Vegetable oil for frying

Instructions:
1. Drain the chickpeas and pat them dry.
2. In a food processor, pulse the chickpeas, onion, parsley, cilantro, garlic, cumin, coriander, salt, and pepper until the mixture is finely ground.
3. Add the flour and water and pulse until just combined.
4. Shape the falafel mixture into 1-inch balls.
5. Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Fry the falafel balls in batches until golden brown and crispy, about 3-4 minutes per batch.
6. Drain the falafel balls on paper towels and serve immediately with your favorite dipping sauce.

Mushroom Penne

Ingredients:
• 1 pound penne pasta
• 1 tablespoon olive oil
• 1 onion, chopped
• 2 cloves garlic, minced
• 1 pound mushrooms, sliced
• 1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley
• 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
• Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

1. Cook the penne pasta according to package directions.
2. While the pasta is cooking, heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
3. Add the garlic and cook for 30 seconds more.
4. Add the mushrooms and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
5. Stir in the parsley and Parmesan cheese.
6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
7. Drain the pasta and add it to the mushroom mixture.
8. Toss to coat.
9. Serve immediately.

Linguine carbonara

INGRIDIENTS:
• 1 pound linguine pasta
• 4 ounces pancetta or guanciale, chopped
• 1/4 cup olive oil
• 4 eggs
• 1 cup grated Pecorino Romano cheese
• 1/2 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
• Fresh black pepper to taste
• 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley

STEPS
1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the linguine according to package directions until al dente. 2. While the pasta is cooking, heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the pancetta or guanciale and cook until crispy, about 5 minutes. 3. In a medium bowl, whisk together the eggs, Pecorino Romano cheese, Parmesan cheese, and black pepper. 4. Drain the pasta and reserve 1 cup of the pasta water. 5. Add the pasta to the skillet with the pancetta or guanciale and toss to coat. 6. Pour the egg mixture over the pasta and toss quickly until the sauce is thickened and coats the pasta. 7. If the sauce is too dry, add a little of the reserved pasta water. 8. Stir in the parsley and serve immediately.

Prawns tagliatelle

Ingredients:
• 1 pound prawns, peeled and deveined • 1/4 cup olive oil • 4 cloves garlic, minced • 1/2 teaspoon dried red pepper flakes

• 1/2 cup dry white wine • 1 (14.5-ounce) can diced tomatoes • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley • Salt and pepper to taste • 1 pound tagliatelle pasta • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions:
1. Cook the tagliatelle according to package directions.
2. While the pasta is cooking, heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the garlic and red pepper flakes and cook for 30 seconds, or until fragrant.
3. Add the prawns and cook for 2-3 minutes per side, or until pink and cooked through.
4. Pour in the white wine and diced tomatoes and bring to a simmer.
5. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 5 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened.
6. Stir in the parsley and season with salt and pepper to taste.
7. Drain the pasta and add it to the skillet with the sauce. Toss to coat.
8. Serve immediately, topped with Parmesan cheese.

Grilled chicken

Ingredients:
• 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
• 2 tablespoons olive oil
• 1 tablespoon lemon juice
• 1 teaspoon dried oregano
• 1/2 teaspoon salt
• 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions

1. Prepare the marinade: In a medium bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, oregano, salt, and pepper.
2. Marinate the chicken: Place the chicken breasts in a shallow dish or zip-top bag and pour the marinade over them. Ensure the chicken is evenly coated with the marinade. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes, or up to 4 hours for maximum flavor.
3. Preheat the grill: Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. If using a gas grill, preheat all burners to medium-high. If using a charcoal grill, light the coals and let them burn until they are mostly gray and covered with a thin layer of ash.
4. Grill the chicken: Remove the chicken from the marinade and pat dry with paper towels. Place the chicken breasts on the preheated grill and cook for 5-6 minutes per side, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C) as measured by a meat thermometer.
5. Rest the chicken: Once cooked, transfer the grilled chicken breasts to a plate and let them rest for 5-10 minutes before slicing. This allows the juices to redistribute, resulting in tender and juicy chicken.
6. Serve and enjoy: Slice the grilled chicken and serve with your favorite sides, such as grilled vegetables, roasted potatoes, or a salad.

Barbeque beef

INGRIDIENTS:
• 1 beef brisket, about 3 pounds
• 1/4 cup olive oil
• 1/4 cup Worcestershire sauce
• 1/4 cup soy sauce
• 1/4 cup Dijon mustard

• 2 tablespoons honey
• 1 tablespoon smoked paprika
• 1 teaspoon onion powder
• 1 teaspoon garlic powder
• 1/2 teaspoon salt
• 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

STEPS
1. Prepare the marinade: In a medium bowl, whisk together the olive oil, Worcestershire sauce, soy sauce, Dijon mustard, honey, smoked paprika, onion powder, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
2. Marinate the brisket: Place the beef brisket in a shallow dish or zip-top bag and pour the marinade over it. Ensure the brisket is evenly coated with the marinade. Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or up to overnight for maximum flavor.
3. Preheat the grill: Preheat your grill to low heat. If using a gas grill, preheat all burners to low. If using a charcoal grill, light the coals and let them burn until they are mostly gray and covered with a thin layer of ash.
4. Smoke the brisket: Place the marinated brisket on the preheated grill and smoke for 3-4 hours, or until the internal temperature reaches 160°F (71°C) as measured by a meat thermometer. This low and slow smoking process will ensure a tender and juicy brisket.
5. Wrap the brisket: Once the internal temperature reaches 160°F (71°C), wrap the brisket tightly in aluminum foil. This will help trap moisture and keep the brisket cooking evenly.
6. Continue cooking: Return the wrapped brisket to the grill and continue cooking for another 2-3 hours, or until the internal temperature reaches 200°F (93°C) as measured by a meat thermometer. This will allow the brisket to break down and become incredibly tender.
7. Rest the brisket: Once cooked, remove the brisket from the grill and let it rest for at least 30 minutes before slicing. This allows the juices to redistribute, resulting in a moist and flavorful brisket.
8. Serve and enjoy: Slice the barbeque beef brisket against the grain and serve with your favorite sides, such as BBQ sauce, coleslaw, and baked beans.
